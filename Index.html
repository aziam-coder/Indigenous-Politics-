About Indigenous Politics

Indigenous politics explores the governance, rights, and challenges faced by Indigenous peoples around the world. It includes discussions about sovereignty, self-determination, and representation.

Key Issues

  • Land rights and resource management
  • Preservation of language and culture
  • Access to education and healthcare
  • Political representation and participation
  • Impact of colonialism and systemic discrimination
